Classes and Workshops

Enid Wood has been teaching workshops at the Mary Nichols Art Center, 301 Burleson, on Saturdays from 1:00 to 4:00. Watch this spot for updates. Contact Enid directly for details. Enid's Website Each workshop costs $30 (or $25 per class if paying for an entire series in advance). This cost includes paper. Please bring your own foam core drawing board, portable easel (optional), and 72 half stick Faber Castell pastels, or 60 Stabilo pastel pencils.
